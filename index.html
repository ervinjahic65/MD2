Frequently Asked Questions

What defines a private billing service?

During a home visit (and select telehealth sessions), you qualify for a Healthcare rebate. In a strict bulk billing model, that rebate is your only income. At {Company}, patients pay an additional gap fee, significantly enhancing your earnings.

Is it possible to bulk bill patients if desired?

Yes, you may. Although our standard gap fee boosts your earnings, you retain the choice to bulk bill any patient at your discretion.

Does private billing imply a quieter service at {Company}?

Not at all. Although some bulk billing providers claim reduced patient flow, the truth is demand is very high—and we often must turn patients away. Consider the following:

  • Our outstanding reputation in home visiting ensures many patients are eager to pay for premium care.
  • Recent closures have diminished after-hours options, dramatically increasing demand for our service.
  • For patients unable or unwilling to pay, cases are assigned to our doctors who may choose to bulk bill—giving you the flexibility to enjoy the best of both approaches.

Am I required to see patients with COVID contacts or symptoms?

No—it’s completely optional. Every patient is screened for COVID before booking. If you opt to see a suspected or confirmed case, full PPE is provided, and you may always offer a telehealth consultation instead.

Can I opt for a telehealth-only shift?

Absolutely. We offer combined home visit/telehealth shifts as well as telehealth-only shifts you can work from home. Telehealth enables you to manage various conditions—including those related to COVID—while earning competitive rates.

What types of conditions will I handle?

You’ll address acute issues typically seen in General Practice or lower-acuity Emergency settings. Our urgent care model means you won’t manage chronic conditions—instead, referrals are made to the patient’s regular GP or the Emergency Department when needed.

As a doctor in training, what support is provided?

A dedicated GP mentor is available around the clock to guide you. You’ll continuously enhance your skills during your time at {Company}, with support offered via phone, video, or image consultation based on each situation.

Why is after-hours care often criticized for billing compliance?

After-hours care has been marred by non-compliant billing practices by some providers, damaging the industry's reputation. This not only undermines trust but also harms both doctors and patients.

What compliance standards should I seek in an after-hours service?

Look for a service where you control your billing decisions, not one that makes them for you. Avoid services that label every after-hours case as urgent. Accurate documentation is vital, so choose a provider with robust triage and record-keeping processes.

How does {Company} ensure compliance with billing standards?

Every process we follow is built with compliance at its core. As a private billing service, we remove the pressure to overbill for high rebates. We educate you thoroughly during onboarding, and our app gives you complete control over billing—simplifying patient triage, accurate code selection, and proper documentation.
